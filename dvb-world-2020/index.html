# DASH-IF at DVB World 2020 DASH-IF is proud and delighted to be invited by the Digital Video Broadcasting (DVB) project to act as a program partner of the DVB's annual flagship conference DVB World in March 2020. DASH-IF is offered to organize a 90min session on the latest technology and deployment advances of Dynamic Adaptive Streaming over HTTP (DASH). More details will follow. DVB World 2020 will take place March 9-11, 2020 in Valencia, Spain. DASH-IF members are invited to participate in the conference at preferantial rates.
